About Us

Cafe Bella offers a unique coffee house environment unlike
any other in COEP. We are not only a place to drop in and get
your morning cup of coffee (although you are more than
welcome to do that), we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy
that tailor-made lunch or dinner. If you need to work, we have a seating
area created specifically for you. If you need to rest, we have a
soft-seating area in front of a stone fire place that is perfect
for your weary mind and body. We offer a delicious variety of
main courses, starters and sweet dishes made by our professionally
trained baristas. We have everything from classic coffee to our
house made specialty beverages. All of our sauces & syrups are made
in-house with all natural ingredients (no chemicals or preservatives)
ensuring you the highest quality in taste & health.
You can complete your dinner/lunch with one of our
delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker.
We look forward to serving you here!
